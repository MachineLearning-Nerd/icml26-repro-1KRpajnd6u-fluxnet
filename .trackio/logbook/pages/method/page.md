## Method

A clean-room NumPy audit exercises deterministic periodic 1D and 2D transport plans. A second audit imports the unmodified author `N`, `L`, and `U` 1D heads from the separately pinned official checkout `ec0cafe3bb48cb7f2497723c5e12c6ebc518442c` and performs small seeded CPU checks.

The D-head is deliberately tested only as a limitation control: averaging one-sided heads conserves mass but is not a universal hard dual-bound mechanism.
